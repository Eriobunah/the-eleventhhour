The Eleventh HourLogistics
— In Time,Every Time

TEH is a CTPAT certified, ISNetworld registered, asset-based freight forwarder and technology platform headquartered in Houston, TX. We are reinventing the freight operating system purpose-built for the most complex, most regulated, highest-value freight in the country.

Who We Are

A Specialty Freight Forwarder Built for Regulated Environments

The Eleventh Hour Logistics was founded on one belief: the most complex, most regulated, most demanding freight in the world — Oil & Gas, HazMat, Pharma, Military, Heavy Equipment — deserves a freight technology platform built to handle it.

Our founder spent years dispatching, managing fleets, and operating in the oilfield freight space before stepping back and watching the technology landscape leave specialized carriers behind. The big platforms chased volume. Nobody was doing this for the verticals that actually needed a purpose-built solution.

TEH is the answer to that gap. We are reinventing the freight space not as a general freight forwarder, but as a vertically integrated platform — built on CTPAT certification, ISNetworld registration, and a WEX fuel card partnership that gives our carriers a genuine financial advantage from day one.
