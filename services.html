Our Services

Freight Solutions Built for
Regulated Environments

From hazardous materials to oilfield equipment, TEH delivers freight services designed for environments where compliance, precision, and reliability are non-negotiable. Every shipment is handled with the diligence required by oil & gas operators and contractors.

Our Freight Services

1

Hazmat Freight Forwarding

Safe and compliant transportation of hazardous materials across all modes: road, intermodal, and air. All shipments handled in accordance with DOT 49 CFR and IATA regulations, with full documentation and regulatory oversight.

2

OCTG & Pipe Logistics

Specialized handling and transport of OCTG, coil, and oilfield materials. TEH provides end-to-end coordination and delivery with precision to support upstream and midstream operations.

3

Customs Clearance & Compliance

End-to-end management of customs documentation, regulatory requirements, and border compliance. TEH ensures shipments move across borders without delays or compliance risks.

4

Cargo Insurance

Comprehensive coverage for high-value and sensitive freight. TEH provides protection options tailored to oil & gas shipments, reducing exposure to risk and potential financial disruption.

5

Warehousing & Distribution

Secure storage, inventory handling, and distribution solutions for oilfield equipment and materials. Facilities and processes are aligned with the demands of energy industry operations.

6

GPS-Tracked Carrier Network

Access to a vetted carrier network with real-time GPS tracking. TEH maintains visibility, accountability, and control across every shipment from origin to destination.
